FAQ

Questions future customers will absolutely ask

Structured like CrossBar's existing product pages, but tightened up for cleaner scanning and better narrative continuity.

What makes PHSM different from a typical hardware wallet?

PHSM is designed around secure MPC computation inside the hardware boundary, paired with CrossBar's ReRAM-based security architecture and FIDO2-ready workflows.

Does PHSM support both self-custody and institutional models?

Yes. The product and software stack are designed to support customizable threshold policies ranging from individual control to delegated or shared custody structures.

Can PHSM be used for authentication as well as asset security?

Yes. CrossBar positions PHSM as a hardware trust anchor for both digital asset protection and phishing-resistant FIDO2 passkey authentication.
